Welcome to Rust!

Why?

  1. ergonomic design
  2. great ecosystem of dev tools/packages
  3. no garbage collection
  4. safety guarantees (no more NullPointerExceptions!)
  5. fearless concurrency (easy multi-threading)

Differences from other languages

  2. macros
  3. mutability
  4. lifetimes
